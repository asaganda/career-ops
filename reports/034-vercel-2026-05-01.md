- **Mitigation:** Frame CanIParkHere as "real-time inference pipeline" and TypescriptQuest as "AI-assisted platform development with prompt engineering." Honest framing, but the gap remains.

**Gap 2: No infra/observability/CI/CD experience**
- **Hard blocker?** Yes — the JD explicitly requires "infrastructure, CI/CD, and observability understanding."
- **Adjacent?** SalonCentric API integrations show systems thinking, but no monitoring/alerting/pipeline work.
- **Mitigation:** None strong enough for a Staff-level role.

**Gap 3: No Next.js / Vercel platform depth**
- **Hard blocker?** Soft blocker (preferred, not required). But a candidate for the Vercel *Agent* team with zero Vercel/Next.js experience is at a disadvantage.
- **Mitigation:** TypescriptQuest could be migrated to Vercel as a quick win, but that feels performative.

**Gap 4: Staff-level architectural influence**
- **Hard blocker?** Yes. $232k–$348k Staff roles expect candidates who have shaped system architecture for teams, not solo side projects.
- **Mitigation:** None without fabrication.
