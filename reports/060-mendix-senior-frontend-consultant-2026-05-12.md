**"Sell senior without lying" plan:**
Not applicable. Even a perfect pitch cannot overcome zero Mendix platform experience when the role is a platform-specific consultant. This is like applying to be a "Senior Salesforce Consultant" without ever touching Salesforce.

**"If downleveled" plan:**
No junior/mid version of this role helps either — the platform expertise requirement doesn't disappear at a lower tier.

**Realistic path:** If Andrew wants to work in the Mendix ecosystem, he would need to:
1. Get a Mendix free tier account and complete their learning path (~4–8 weeks)

3. Apply to junior consulting roles or Mendix partner companies, not Mendix's own Expert Services team at senior level
